Repeating events (During the semester)

Graduate Student Forum

The GSF provides a platform for students to present research ideas, practice presentations (job talks, conference presentations, etc.) and learn about other research in the department in an informal setting.

Time/Place
Once a month during the colloquium, Thursdays from 2-3pm in DLC 170
Format
The format of GSF will generally be short talks where students can receive feedback on their research ideas or just want to share interesting research. Students can also practice job talks or conference presentations, and practice their “elevator pitch.”
Contact
csgsa@colorado.edu
